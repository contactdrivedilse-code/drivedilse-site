1. Lease Agreement for Self-Drive

This document is an electronic record under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and complies with provisions of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Section 51) which recognises leasing of vehicles for personal use. This agreement is generated digitally and does not require a physical signature.

By confirming a booking on DriveDilSe ("Platform"), the Guest (you) enters into a lease agreement with DriveDilSe ("Lessor") for the specified vehicle for the duration of the Booking Period.

This is a lease — not a transfer of ownership. The vehicle remains the absolute property of DriveDilSe / its registered owner at all times. The Guest holds the vehicle as trustee during the Booking Period.

Key Definitions

  • Booking Period — from the confirmed start date/time to the confirmed end date/time.
  • Primary Guest — the person who created the booking and completed KYC verification.
  • Co-Driver — an additional registered driver added to the booking via the Platform.
  • Permitted Territory — entire territory of India, excluding Ladakh and international borders (Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, Pakistan).
  • Designated Location — the pickup/drop address specified in the booking confirmation.

2. Booking Policy

Booking Confirmation

A booking is confirmed only upon full payment of the Booking Fee and Security Deposit through the Platform. The Booking Period commences on the Booking Start Date irrespective of whether the Guest takes delivery.

Delivery of Vehicle

The Primary Guest or registered Co-Driver must be present in person to receive the vehicle and must produce:

  • Original valid driving licence
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Additional address proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, or valid Passport)

The Guest must inspect the vehicle (exterior walk-around + interior check) before accepting delivery. Any pre-existing damage must be reported immediately. Post-acceptance, the vehicle condition is the Guest's responsibility.

Booking Extension

Extensions must be requested and confirmed through the Platform before the Booking End Date. Unauthorised use of the vehicle beyond the Booking Period will attract late-return penalties as per the Fee Policy.

Return of Vehicle

The vehicle must be returned to the Designated Location with all Vehicle Documentation, keys, key fob, and in-vehicle devices in the same condition as at the time of delivery (normal wear and tear excepted).

Returning the vehicle at a location other than the Designated Location will incur transportation costs charged to the Guest.

3. Vehicle Usage Policy

Permitted Allowed Uses

  • Personal travel within the Permitted Territory (India, excluding Ladakh and international borders)
  • Driving only by the Primary Guest or verified, registered Co-Driver
  • Use in compliance with all applicable traffic laws and the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988

Prohibited Strictly Forbidden Uses

The following constitute a material breach of the lease agreement:

  • Commercial use — transporting passengers or goods for hire or reward
  • Providing driving lessons
  • Use by any person other than the Primary Guest or registered Co-Driver
  • Racing, rallies, off-road events, endurance tests
  • Towing or pushing another vehicle or trailer
  • Any criminal, illegal, or unlawful activity
  • Transporting hazardous, combustible, or overweight goods
  • Travel outside the Permitted Territory
  • Driving under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any intoxicant
  • Using a mobile phone while driving (hands-free or otherwise)
  • Smoking, tobacco, or alcohol use inside the vehicle
  • Transporting animals
  • Exceeding the vehicle's rated passenger capacity
  • Jump-starting the vehicle without prior written consent
  • Tampering with or removing in-vehicle tracking/safety devices

Any prohibited use voids insurance coverage and renders the Guest solely liable for all resulting damages, costs, and legal consequences.

Guest Obligations During Booking

  • Maintain vehicle cleanliness and operating condition throughout the Booking Period
  • Maintain a valid, current driving licence at all times
  • Notify DriveDilSe immediately if driving licence is suspended or revoked
  • Keep vehicle doors locked and windows closed when parked
  • Not modify, alter, or repair the vehicle without written consent
  • Keep vehicle free from any liens, claims, or encumbrances
  • Ensure all original Vehicle Documentation remains in the vehicle at all times

4. Security Deposit Policy

A refundable Security Deposit is charged at the time of booking as specified in the booking details. The deposit is held by DriveDilSe until the vehicle is returned in satisfactory condition.

Refund Conditions

  • Full refund — vehicle returned on time, at Designated Location, in original condition, with all documentation, with no outstanding dues
  • Partial / No refund — outstanding Booking Fee, damage charges, traffic fines, fuel shortfall, cleaning charges, or late return penalties will be deducted from the deposit

Forfeiture

  • If the Guest cancels after booking and before taking delivery, the Security Deposit and Booking Fee may be forfeited per the Cancellation Policy
  • If DriveDilSe cancels due to no fault of the Guest, the Security Deposit and Booking Fee will be fully refunded

Security Deposit refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after vehicle return and inspection.

5. Damage, Accident & Theft Policy

The Guest assumes sole financial liability for the vehicle during the entire Booking Period. DriveDilSe shall not be liable for any loss or damage suffered by the Guest or any third party arising from the use of the vehicle.

Immediate Obligations on Damage / Accident / Theft

Within 24 hours of any incident, the Guest must:

  1. Notify DriveDilSe in writing with full details of the incident
  2. File an FIR at the nearest police station and provide DriveDilSe a copy
  3. Take the vehicle to the workshop specified by DriveDilSe (for accidents/damage)
  4. Provide full cooperation for insurance claim processing

Failure to fulfil these obligations makes the Guest solely liable for all resulting costs and consequences, including loss of insurance coverage.

Financial Liability

  • Deliberate damage / Prohibited Use — Guest bears 100% of all costs including third-party claims, repair costs, and insurance declared value if claim is rejected
  • Accidental / Unintentional damage — Guest pays only the amount exceeding insurance coverage (excess/deductible as per the applicable policy)
  • Theft (vehicle recovered) — Agreement terminates; Guest pays all unpaid dues and costs incurred
  • Total loss — Guest liable for shortfall between insurance payout and book value of vehicle, plus any unpaid dues

Vehicle Condition at Return

Normal wear and tear is acceptable. Damage beyond normal wear and tear will be assessed and charged as per the Damage Policy, including costs to restore the vehicle to its original condition.

The Guest is solely responsible for all traffic violations, toll evasion, and related fines incurred during the Booking Period, including:

  • Unauthorised parking
  • Speeding and red-light violations
  • Toll non-payment / FASTag deductions
  • Interstate road tax / permit charges
  • Any other violations under the Motor Vehicles Act

Reporting Obligation

Any traffic violation must be reported to DriveDilSe in writing within 24 hours of occurrence (or at least 24 hours before any court date). The Guest must settle fines directly with the relevant authority and provide DriveDilSe proof of payment.

If the Guest fails to pay, DriveDilSe reserves the right to pay on the Guest's behalf and recover the amount from the Security Deposit or by other means.

Vehicle Confiscation / Seizure

If the vehicle is confiscated or impounded during the Booking Period, the Guest must notify DriveDilSe within 24 hours and provide full cooperation for its release. All costs of release, fines, and penalties shall be borne by the Guest.
