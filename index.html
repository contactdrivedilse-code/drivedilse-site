Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your first drive.

You need a valid Aadhaar Card and a Driving Licence. These are uploaded once during profile verification and carry over to all future bookings.
The minimum booking duration is 6 hours. Pricing is calculated proportionally, so you only pay for the time you actually use.
No. All prices shown are GST-inclusive with no hidden fees. The only additional liability is the Rs 2,000 damage deductible, charged only if a new damage occurs during your trip.
Yes! Rates improve automatically: 5% off for 2–3 days, 8% for 4–5 days, 10% for 6–7 days, 15% for up to 14 days, 20% for up to 21 days, and 25% for longer trips. All with 18% GST already included.
After booking and KYC verification, our representative meets you at handover and gives you a Check-In OTP. At return, a Check-Out OTP closes the trip. This ensures a secure, dispute-free process every time.
Confirmed bookings can be cancelled from your profile. Extensions can be booked and paid for in-app while your trip is active. For other changes, reach out on WhatsApp — we respond fast.
